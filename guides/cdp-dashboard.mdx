---
title: "Get a CDP URL from the Dashboard"
description: "Start a browser and copy its CDP URL"
---

## Steps

1) Sign in to the Dashboard

2) Open “Browser Playground”

3) Click “Start Browser”

4) Copy the “CDP URL” shown on the page

5) Use it in your app (see [/guides/cdp-playwright](/guides/cdp-playwright))

## Managing sessions

- Visit “Browser Logs” to see active and completed sessions
- Stop any session from the table to revoke its CDP URL
